General info
This translation service is created by the NLP research group at the Institute of Computer Science, University of Tartu. The aim of this service is to demonstrate the translation quality and to collect anonymous evaluation bits via the blind "choose the best translation" option.
This project is supported by NPELT . The text you feed to the system can be saved and/or used for research purposes.
